About MoveGoo

The story, mission, and people behind your seamless journeys.

Our Story

Founded in 2025, MoveGoo was born from a simple idea: to make transportation and logistics easy, reliable, and accessible for everyone. Operating under our registered company name, **Nayak and Nayak Kokila Travels and VIP logistic pvt. ltd.**, we started with a mission to bridge the gaps in the travel industry. From a small office in Odisha, we have grown into a trusted partner for thousands of customers, offering a comprehensive suite of services from car rentals to nationwide logistics. Our journey is fueled by a passion for service and a commitment to moving you forward.
